    The Superior Story

    In 2013, the young visionary entrepreneur Mrs. Bharathi started Superior Systems in Bengaluru on the fundamentals of implementing innovations to improve the life style through technology solutions and drive the progress of clients. Since 2013, we have been focusing to grow as customer centric organization based on our values and commitments. Our dedicated relevant teams address the needs of enterprise and corporate customers. And due expertise of individuals in our organization, we have been able to cater to the needs and requirements of our customers across the geographical region we operate. And due to fidelity of our team towards clients, organization has grown exponentially since the year of incorporation. Our company has recorded the growth in terms of turnover and revenue by 3 times in FY 2015-16 as compared to FY 2014-15. Apple India has acknowledged the growth our organization by appointing us as Regional Distributor for their range of products across the respective region we have been operating.

    The Transformation

    During late 2016, Mr. Sachidananda K, Director of Superior Tele Mobiles Private Limited with his predetermined ambitions transformed M/s Superior Systems as M/s Superior Tele Mobiles Private Limited. And this transformation was made to meet the demands of growing business opportunities as recommended by our esteemed clientele.

    The Corporate Overview

    Superior has evolved as a medium to bridge the gap between the technology manufactures and its customers. We understand the technological needs of individual as well as enterprise customers and provide them the unique and customized solution to challenge the current competitive business environment and add value to their line of business. Our expert tem is capable of defining requirements of our customers by perceiving their road-maps to grow as an organization or as an intellectual. We create value to the institution across industries such as financial, manufacturing, health care, educational, logistics, business services and many more. We have expertise and clear understanding of the challenges faced by the organizations in the implementation of reliable technology solution provided by the technological partners. With all these practical know-how, we have gained recognition as trusted partners of Apple, LG, Microsoft, Epson etc. With the help of our corporate team we drive transformation and improve productivity of our clients and emerge as market leaders.
